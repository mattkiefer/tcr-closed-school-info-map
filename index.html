Following a trend in New York City, Philadelphia and elsewhere, black communities are most affected by public school closures. Three years after Chicago’s mass closures, black neighborhoods are still stuck with the most vacant schools, with Austin and West Englewood at the top of the list.
Race, education, home vacancy and poverty data compiled from 2014 American Community Survey 5-year data. Crime records reported by Chicago Police Department between September 2015 through August 2016.
